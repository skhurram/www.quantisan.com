Sold all my CAF @ \$32.35 (fee \$5) for a sizable gain (in at \$24.02) in just a few days. And 20%+ in today alone! The price bounced back right at the 50 dma. Furthermore, there's a lot of talk about the central banks saving the financials. DJIA moved about 900 points in past two days already. Lastly, today is a quadruple witching Friday, so I don't want to leave the gain on the table with the expected volatility.

In any case, I've stuck to my target of leaving at about \$30 and/or when the price spikes up.
