About

My name is Paul Lam. I am the technical co-founder at Glassy Media, a startup based in Cambridge, Massachusetts. We add data and technology to scale the manual process of public relation. I am also a co-organizer of the Boston Clojure Group.

From 2010 to 2013, I was a data scientist at uSwitch.com in London, UK. Before 2010, I was in Toronto, Canada as an algorithmic trading system developer for family fund managers and a biomedical engineer at Industry Canada. Prior to that, I worked on the power electronics for the International Space Station as well as the Airbus A380. I write about my experience building a startup, data science, computing, and miscellaneous thoughts on this site.
