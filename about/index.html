About

My name is Paul Lam. I love identifying problems and building things to solve them. I write about data science, computing, personal notes, and topics on financial trading.

I am a Co-founder at Glassy Media, a startup based in Boston, MA. We are building a tool to increase information access and transparency. Previously I was a data scientist at uSwitch.com in London, a contractor for startups to build distributed data systems to make large amount of data accessible, an algorithmic trading system developer, and a biomedical engineer.
