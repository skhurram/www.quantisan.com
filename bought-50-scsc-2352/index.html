BOUGHT +50 SCSC @23.52

I placed my stop at \$22.70 as planned today and it was triggered at 10:41 am. The exit price ended up at \$23.52 from a quick run. But SCSC further pushed along and closed at \$24.14.

I should have noticed from the 200 week MA that SCSC has been pretty strong lately. I should have only entered when the long term trend is in sync with my position.

Anyway, after running through a few more charts, it seems that my stock filter is giving a contrarian indication. There's still a lot of work to do to develop this new system.
